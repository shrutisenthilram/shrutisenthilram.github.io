Shruti is a product designer who is currently working at UC San Diego.

Hi, I'm a Product Designer and UX Researcher pursuing a double major in Computer Science and Business Economics at UC San Diego. My journey in design started with a curiosity about how technology can solve real-world problems and improve people's daily lives. Over time, I realized that the key to impactful design is understanding the people who use the products, listening to their needs, and translating those insights into intuitive, meaningful experiences.

At UC San Diego, I have had the opportunity to combine technical and analytical skills from Computer Science with strategic thinking from Business Economics. This combination allows me to approach design challenges with both creativity and a practical perspective, balancing user needs with business objectives. I enjoy exploring every stage of the product lifecycle, from identifying user pain points and conducting research to ideating solutions, prototyping, and delivering polished, high-fidelity designs.

Collaboration is at the heart of my work. I thrive when partnering with engineers, product managers, and researchers to iterate on ideas and ensure that solutions are not only visually compelling but also functional, accessible, and genuinely helpful to users. My toolkit includes Figma, FigJam, prototyping, and interaction design, which I use to experiment with micro-interactions, flows, and interfaces that enhance usability and create delight.

Beyond the tools and techniques, I approach every project with curiosity, empathy, and a growth mindset. I am constantly seeking new ways to learn, improve, and understand the people who will use the products I design. My goal is to build tools and experiences that make a real difference, improve collaboration, and help users accomplish their goals efficiently and joyfully.
